About Us

Charan Security is a cybersecurity solution, training, and research company founded in the year 2019, and is recognized by ISO 9001:2015. Today, as more and more data is becoming digital and accessible through wireless or wired digital communication networks and across the omnipresent internet, we need some acute measures to safeguard this information. Businesses suffer from cyber-attacks in the form of data breaches, making the company's reputation and customer experience degrade either due to lack of knowledge or negligence of the cyber security solutions, which ultimately results in greater loss.

Here, at Charan Security, we offer a wide variety of measures in cyber security solutions to make your business safe and secure online so that our customer conducts their daily business with a flawless level of security. This is clearly depicted by our Company’s vision.
